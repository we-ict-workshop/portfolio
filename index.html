About

Curious about how people and models understand each other.

I'm a Teaching Assistant and M.Sc. student in Computer Science and Engineering at the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), currently working on my thesis. My work sits at the intersection of Human–Computer Interaction and Explainable AI — I build interfaces and models that stay understandable even as they get more complex, whether that's a protein structure predictor, a mobile eye-disease classifier, or a parser for low-resource languages like Bangla.

I completed my B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), where my thesis on Bangla dependency parsing became my first first-author publication. Since then I've published across HCI, NLP, medical imaging and software engineering venues, and I still enjoy the moment a messy dataset turns into a working system someone can actually trust.
